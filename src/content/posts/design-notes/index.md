## Start with rhythm

Readable pages are built on steady rhythm: consistent spacing, clear type hierarchy, and predictable alignment.

### Practical checklist

- Use generous line height for paragraphs.
- Keep headings short and descriptive.
- Let whitespace do most of the work.

## Color as a whisper

Color should guide, not shout. A soft accent color is often enough to help navigation and highlight key ideas.

## Finish with restraint

Ship the simplest version that feels complete, then iterate with data and feedback.
