## Move with intent

Rapid shipping is only useful when the direction is clear. Slow shipping makes time for validation.

## Build feedback loops

Short check-ins and lightweight prototypes can prevent bigger rework later.

### Signals to watch

- Are users asking the same question repeatedly?
- Does onboarding feel heavy?

## Keep the surface clean

Every new feature deserves a cleanup pass to keep the experience readable and calm.
