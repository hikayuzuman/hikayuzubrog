## Capture first

Store raw notes quickly before they vanish. A bullet list is enough for the first draft.

## Shape later

Convert notes into sections with clear headings. This step is about structure, not style.

### Prompts that help

- What should the reader remember tomorrow?
- Which part felt hardest to explain?

## Publish consistently

Small, regular posts are easier to maintain than rare, giant posts.
